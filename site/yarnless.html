    UPCYCLED FASHION TO SUIT YOUR LIFESTYLE

    In a world where consumerism is ripe and second hand fashion lacks convenience, Yarnless thinks it's time to ditch the shopping spree, the sales and the high street fast fashion for an ethical but personalized way of shopping.

    THE PROBLEM

    Personalization and fashion app's mega filters have allowed top brands to bring convenient shopping to the masses. With a click of a button, fashion labels know your fit, favourite colours, age, style and budget. Although convenient, the fast fashion industry provides this at the expense of the people in the production line and the environment around us. Second hand shopping and thrift stores need to step up with a plan to make their shopping experiences more convenient and their presence more powerful in the marketplace.

    THE OBJECTIVE

    With yarnless, we hope to bring personalization and convenience to second hand shopping. Everything that makes you favourite online brand enjoyable to shop for is now going to be true for the user that wants to thrift from the convenience of the train seat or their local coffee spot.

    THE SOLUTION Yarnless is an app that helps create a personalized profile for you and filters your feed to fit your interests, needs and style. With a wider range of fashion that is being pulled in from hundreds of thrift stores, Yarnless offers you plenty options but with the ability to filter them easily. Quite the opposite of your local second hand shop with a varied and odd mix of garments on the racks.
